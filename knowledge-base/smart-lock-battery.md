# Smart Lock Battery Issues

Product: CasaIQ Smart Lock  
Category: Troubleshooting

## Common Symptoms

- Lock does not respond
- Keypad light is dim
- App shows low battery
- Lock disconnects intermittently

## Troubleshooting Steps

1. Replace all four AA batteries with new alkaline batteries.
2. Wait 30 seconds.
3. Press the reset button once.
4. Open the CasaIQ app and check device status.
5. Restart the Wi-Fi bridge if the lock remains offline.

## Escalation Conditions

Escalate if:

- The customer is locked out.
- The lock is overheating.
- The customer reports a security concern.
- Troubleshooting fails twice.

## Response Guideline

Apologize, ask if the customer is currently locked out, then provide battery replacement steps.
